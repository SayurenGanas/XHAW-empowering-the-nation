Our Story of Empowerment

Transforming lives through skills development and education

Founded by Precious Radetie

Empowering the Nation was born from Precious Radetie's personal experience and deep commitment to her community. Witnessing how her parents and other elderly relatives were never given opportunities for formal education or skills development inspired her to create meaningful change.

Precious recognized the untapped potential in domestic workers and gardeners - hardworking individuals who form the backbone of many households but often lack access to professional training that could enhance their employability and earning potential.

Every person deserves the chance to develop their skills and build a better future. Education is the most powerful tool we have to break cycles of limited opportunity.

- Precious Radetie, Founder

Founder's Vision

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide professional skills training that enhances employability, increases earning potential, and enables entrepreneurship among domestic workers and gardeners in our communities.

Our Vision

To create a nation where every individual has access to quality skills development, transforming households and communities through empowered, skilled professionals.

Our Promise

To deliver practical, market-relevant training that makes domestic workers and gardeners more competitive in the job market while fostering entrepreneurial spirit.
