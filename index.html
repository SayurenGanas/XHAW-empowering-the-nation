About Our Mission

Empowering the Nation was founded by Precious Radetie to provide skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Our mission is to support community members who were never given the chance to upskill themselves or follow formal educational qualifications.

Through our comprehensive training programs, we help domestic workers and gardeners become more marketable when seeking employment, enabling them to earn higher wages. Our graduates also have the opportunity to start their own small businesses using their newly acquired skills.
